Advanced Item Raffler
An elaborate contraption that can dispense raffle tickets and then deliver a reward to the raffle winner. This model can dispense up to 100 raffle tickets for up to 8 items (or item stacks) and/or a cash prize. The raffle tickets can be free or can cost Councils. This model has a place for an inscription that is displayed to each user, and a message that is shown only to the winner. This model can also be configured to have a time limit of 1-60 minutes.
Value: 100
Stack: 10
