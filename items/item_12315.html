"Kur Hauntings" by Zuk-Ghu
This is a real treasure -- an original hand-copied manuscript by Zuk-Ghu, the orcish poet of Kur! Mocked by his people, Zuk-Ghu was forced to write and distribute his poems in secrecy. They are truly beautiful, though... if you're fluent in Orc. If you only know pidgin-Orc like most people, these poems are largely inscrutable.
