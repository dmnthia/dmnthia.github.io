On Becoming Great Again
A ponderous volume wherein an anonymous rakshasa mage explores the idea that the 'old' rakshasa form is somehow still locked within each 'new' rakshasa body. The author posits that certain potions might do the trick, and then explores numerous alchemical hypotheses, none of which pan out too well, but one complex recipe catches your eye.
