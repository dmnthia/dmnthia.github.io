Potent Stoneskin Potion
This potion can teach you what it's like to have fluid stone instead of skin. It's an unpleasant sensation but its combat efficacy is impressive.
For 15 minutes, gain +10 mitigation against slashing, piercing, crushing, fire, cold, and electricity damage, both direct and indirect, and -16 mitigation vs acid. Also gain -1 Movement Speed and +20 Unarmed damage.
