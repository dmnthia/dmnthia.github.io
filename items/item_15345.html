Calmweed
A packet of strange chemicals wrapped in a leaf. While it has minor benefits, its primary use is to cure Bounceweed hangovers. It can also temporarily relieve the symptoms of Bounceweed abuse. Calmweed is not addictive.
For 60 minutes Max Power is raised by +3, all animal attacks deal +2 damage, and negative side-effects of Bounceweed use are masked
Value: 600
Stack: 10
