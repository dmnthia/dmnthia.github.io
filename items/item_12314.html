"Collected Works of Gammo"
A heavy volume of poems by the dwarven master Gammo. It even includes the poem "My Penis Is The Sunstone", which has been censored by the Dwarven Culture Group for over 500 years! This is a great find for poetry lovers, though dwarves may find it too embarrassing to own. Less prudish individuals tend to find Gammo's works uplifting and ennervating.
