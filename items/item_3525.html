The Alchemist's Screams
An orcish Hal-Ku story (which are non-fiction accounts of orcs torturing other people to death). Recounts every minute of a six-week torture session, wherein the orcish author slowly killed a human alchemist. Much of the book is padded with grotesque anecdotes, but one useful recipe is hidden in the appendix.
