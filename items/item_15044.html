Persona Minimizer Potion
The florid smell of rotting mushroom gives this potion that special something. Enjoy!
For 15 minutes, all your attacks will seem to do 25% less damage to the target for purposes of determining who the target chooses to attack. (As a result, it may choose to attack someone else, unless nobody else is around.)
