Aberration Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of creatures made by mad science or dark chemistry.

Aberration Phrenology

An understanding of how sentient aberrations think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Alchemy

The art of combining esoteric things together to make potions and poisons.

Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of creatures in general. This is an umbrella skill that encompasses a vast suite of sub-skills for particular types of creatures. This skill represents generalized knowledge that applies to all creatures in the world.

Animal Handling

The art of taming animals and teaching them to fight alongside you.

Archery

Expertise with using bows to hunt and kill.

Armor Augmentation

Knowledge of the techniques for creating Chest-Armor Augments and Leg-Armor Augments. (Augments for other types of equipment are covered by other skills.)

Armor Patching

The ability to use repair kits to fix up your armor.

Art History

Understanding of the rich history of art in Alharth. This skill can help you identify valuable artwork, but more importantly, it helps you to be a well-rounded individual.

Arthropod Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of spiders, insects, and scorpions.

Artistry

Skill in the visual arts, including painting, sculpting, and mosaic creation.

Bear and Bugbear Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of bears. Bugbears, which are half-goblin and half-bear, are also covered by this skill, as they take after their bear side more than the goblin side.

Beast Metabolism

Represents your metabolic efficiency when consuming raw meats.

Buckle Artistry

Skill in creating belt buckles with symbolic meanings. Belt buckle artwork is a deeply personal expression which can give hammer warriors a morale boost in battle. However, because they are so personal, crafted belt buckles are only useful to the person who crafts them.

Butchering

Skill in the act of removing meat and useful body organs from a corpse. You need to be carrying a Butcher Knife in order to butcher a corpse. Corpses of sentient creatures, such as goblins and humans, can't be butchered.

Calligraphy

The art of writing things. Handy for... writing things. It's also a valuable art for swordsmen and other warriors, who can infuse their art with potent additional effects.

Canine Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of dogs, wolves, and werewolves. Gives bonuses to killing, skinning, and raising these creatures.

Carpentry

Preparing and treating lumber, and making things out of wood.

Cartography

Umbrella skill for map-related activities.

Cheesemaking

Making cheese, butter, and other dairy products. But mostly: cheese.

Civic Pride

Represents your willingness to tackle the small, mundane tasks in your community, and your understanding of why that's important. This skill is especially valued by humans.

Construct Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of constructs such as golems.

Cooking

Skill in preparing food for consumption.

Corpse Talking

Ability to commune telepathically with nearby dead bodies. Higher skill grants higher chances of persuading recalcitrant spirits, as well as higher comprehension rates when learning from the dead.

Crone-Kin Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of crones and their ilk, including harpies, hags, and striga.

Crone-Kin Phrenology

An understanding of how crones think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Deer

The art of living and fighting as a deer.

Demon Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of demons.

Demon Phrenology

An understanding of how demons think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Dinosaur Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of dinosaurs. This skill also covers birds, which are descended from dinosaurs, and dragons, which are descended from birds.

Druid

Druidin'

Dye Making

Skill in producing dye. These dyes can be used to color armor. This skill covers only the creation of the dye; Textile Creation, Tanning, or Blacksmithing is used to apply the dye, depending on what the armor is made of.

Dying

The art of dying.

Elemental Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of elementals.

Elf Phrenology

An understanding of how elves think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Elven Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of elves.

Endurance

The ability to endure heavy loads and uncomfortable situations without getting tired. Your Endurance increases the speed of your armor regeneration between fights, among other things. (The most common way to raise Endurance is simply to take armor damage in combat.)

Fae Phrenology

An understanding of how fairies think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Fey Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of faeries and fey creatures, including some animals that look mundane but aren't, such as the fey panther.

Fire Magic

Using Fire to destroy your enemies. This is a very potent offensive skill, including long-range and area-effect attacks, and even some Cold attacks for versatility. However, there are drawbacks: Fire Magic is extremely Power-intensive; all creatures generate extra Rage when they are burned; and many creatures have some resistance to fire.

First Aid

The ability to use first aid kits to heal yourself or others.

Fish and Snail Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of fish. Also snails. They're very similar. Really.

Fishing

The art of collecting fish.

Fletching

The craft of arrow-making. Most fletching recipes require elements obtained from Carpentry, so it's often handy to know both skills well. At higher level, fletchers also need various Alchemy components.

Foraging

The art of picking things up. Except mushrooms, those are special for some reason. Experts at Foraging are also experts at tracking down stationary items and locations (such as many Quest Objectives).

Fungoid Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of fungus people, slime monsters, overgrown bacteria, and ooze beasts.

Gardening

Growing things in a garden: herbs, food, and flowers.

Gender Studies

A careful exploration of gender roles and sexuality, focusing on Human, Elven, and Raksasha cultures. This knowledge is not directly applicable to combat or crafting, but it helps you to be a well-rounded individual.

Geology

Digging crystals out of the ground, and knowing what they do.

Giant Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of giant-kin such as yetis and ogres.

Giant Bat

Skill in living and fighting as a bat.

Giant Phrenology

An understanding of how humans think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Goblin Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of goblins.

Goblin Phrenology

An understanding of how goblins think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Goblinese

Fluency in the language of goblins.

Gourmand

The art of appreciating what you eat. A high gourmand skill allows you to get more benefits from fancier foods. Each food has a Gourmet Level, which caps your benefits from this skill. If you eat a food with a Gourmet Level lower than your Gourmand skill, you will get benefits as if your skill was the food's Gourmet Level.

Hammer

Mastery of the use of hammers and clubs in combat. Power consumption is higher than average, but so is damage. Hammers excel at rapidly murdering enemies, no more and no less.

Hoplology

The study of weapons and armor, with a focus on the benefits and weaknesses of different kinds of equipment. As the skill increases, Staff users will find that their combat abilities also restore Armor.

Howling

Skill in howling as a werewolf.

Human Phrenology

An understanding of how humans think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Ice Magic

A specialization of Fire Magic that focuses entirely upon lowering temperature instead of raising it. It has protective and controlling abilities as well as offensive abilities.

Incorporeal Creature Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of incorporeal creatures, such as ghosts and shadow illusions.

Industry

Competence in completing tasks to specification. Highly industrious workers can juggle more tasks at once, and earn slightly higher rewards for their hard work.

Iocaine Resistance

Practiced resistance to the deadly mushroom poison known as Iocaine. (Each point of this skill reduces the damage from Iocaine-based poisons by 1%.)

Jewelry Augmentation

Knowledge of the techniques for creating Necklace Augments and Ring Augments. (Augments for other types of equipment are covered by other skills.)

Leatherworking

Turning leathers into armor and goods.

Lore

Mastery of ancient knowledge.

Lycanthropy

Skill in fighting as a wolf beast. Also indicates your ability to control and suppress the beast mode.

Meditation

Skill in centering yourself and creating inner focus. This is especially helpful when using Unarmed combat skill. To meditate, you must be near a Meditation Pillar. Each meditation also requires a simple focus item, such as a fruit or flower.

Mentalism

The power of mind over matter: psychic powers! This combat skill focuses on recovery and restoration, but it also has multiple damaging abilities.

Mining

Digging metals and ores out of the ground.

Mushroom Farming

The specialized art of growing mushrooms in special grow-boxes.

Myconic

Knowledge of the secret language of ancient fungus creatures.

Necromancy

Necromancy is control over the undead, along with other powers that trifle with mortality. To use necromantic abilities, you must be wielding or wearing a necromancy gem. You can often find these on weapons, and less often on pieces of armor. (If you are in a beast form, necromancy gems in weapons won't work, but ones in armor will.)

Non-Ruminant Ungulate Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of large herbivores that do NOT chew cud, such as pigs, rhinos, and horses.

Notoriety

A measure of your renown. You earn Notoriety the first time you defeat certain enemies (typically any monster with a proper name), and for completing particularly difficult quests.

Orc Phrenology

An understanding of how orcs think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Pathology

Skill in figuring out how someone or something died. Higher skill allows more details to be determined and more difficult organisms to be examined.

Percussion Instruments

Skill in performing with percussion instruments such as drums, bells, and dulcimers.

Performance

This umbrella skill represents your ability to entertain with performances of music, acrobatics, dance, art, recitals, and so on.

Pig

The art of living and fighting as a pig.

Plant Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of monstrous plants.

Poetry Appreciation

Skill in finding the meaning of poems, especially difficult and tedious ones.

Productivity

(unused)

Rakshasa Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of rakshasa.

Rakshasa Phrenology

An understanding of how rakshasa think and feel, based on careful study of the shape of their skulls. (Phrenology skills provide critical-hit chances to all Psychology and Mentalism attacks against the corresponding type of creature. Phrenology also has more esoteric uses.)

Reptile Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of snakes, snake-people, and generally snakey things.

Rodent Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of rodents.

Ruminant Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of cud-chewing herbivores such as cows, sheep, deer, and camels.

Shield

Expertise in the use of a shield, both as a weapon and as a defensive tool.

Sigil Scripting

Using magical inks and arcane rituals to create areas of power.

Skinning

Skill in the act of removing fur or skin from an animal. You need to be carrying a Skinning Knife in order to skin a corpse. Corpses of sentient creatures, such as goblins and humans, can't be skinned.

Spider

The art of living and fighting as a spider.

Staff

Mastery of the staff as a martial weapon. Staff fighting has a mix of both offensive and defensive abilities.

Stone Working

The use of stone in construction and decoration.

Surveying

Analyzing the land to locate valuable resources or important locations. Surveying has many uses, but is most commonly used to make resource maps for Geology and Mining.

Sword

Expertise with swords, rapiers, and the like. Swords are very damaging, and can reduce an opponent's Rage.

Tailoring

Tailoring has two aspects: first, you use it to turn textiles into clothing. Second, you can use tailoring to make clothing and leather armor more cofortable, more useful, or more stylish.

Tanning

The craft of turning disgusting animal skins, furs, and hides into leather, fleece, and other material ready to be used in clothing and armor.

Teleportation

The magical art of suddenly being somewhere else.

Textile Creation

The craft of turning raw ingredients into cloth. Textile creation includes cotton, wool, silk, and many other fiber types. Cloth made from animal hides (e.g. leather) comes from the Tanning skill instead.

Toolcrafting

Skill in the creation of advanced tools and unusual equipment. Far more than just creating hand tools, an expert Toolcrafter is a master of repurposing found materials, and can create belts, armors, and even weapons from unusual materials. This equipment is usually designed to meet very specialized needs.

Transmutation

The science of permanently altering how a magical item works. Also covers manipulation of the basic materials of magic, such as phlogiston.

Treasure Cartography

Experience creating maps from old clues. The maps can lead to treasure... or tragedy.

Undead Anatomy

Knowledge of the physiology of undead, such as skeletons, zombies, and liches. This skill doesn't cover undead without bodies, such as ghosts and wraiths.

Vocabulary

Knowledge of many words in the common language.

Wind Instruments

Skill in performing with brass and woodwind instruments such as trumpets, ocarinas, and flutes.
